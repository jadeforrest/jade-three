# Song Q&A

## What's the song about?

It's about the gap between what you feel inside and what you say out loud to the world around you. The title was deliberate — I wanted the contrast between the name and the music to do some work. The song itself is pretty and delicate, which felt like exactly the right response to the title.

## How did it come together in the studio/production?

It's mostly instrumental, though there are some textural vocal parts — no lyrics. I used filters on the drums to make a kind of shifting background sound, so there's a lot of movement. That combined with the melody made for a kind of pretty, delicate tone, I thought. There were some droning sounds that managed to sound pretty too. I also used some acid and synth sounds to move things around, and layered drums. I'm really happy with how it turned out.

## Favourite moment in the track

I loved when the male vocals came in a little past the halfway point. They really sealed the emotional tenderness of the song for me.

## One thing listeners might miss

I'd encourage a careful listen at the beginning. I like the rhythmic textures right from the start — you might too!
