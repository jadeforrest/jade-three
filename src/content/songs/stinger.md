# Song Q&A

## What's the song about?

Instrumental. The overall feeling is floaty, beautiful, melodic.

## Where did the idea come from?

The approach was to select the mood first, then find sounds that matched it — and layer them up to reinforce that feeling.

## How did it come together in the studio/production?

Used a lot of loops — went through a large library finding sounds that matched a particular feeling. Then a lot of experimentation, moving things around until it sounded right. Added some acid sounds to balance out the floatiness and ground it a little. That contrast was something I was really happy with. Stinger was probably the first song I was really happy with.

## On the title

Tried to find something that felt a little opposite to the sound. Came across an instrument called a stinger bass and liked how that sounded as a name.

## On releasing it

Even doing it in public, I had no expectation that anyone was actually listening — which made it feel safe (and honestly, I still feel pretty safe in that regard). But it felt good, and I got some really positive feedback from people who actually know something about music. That gave me the incentive to keep going.
