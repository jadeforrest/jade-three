# Song Q&A

## What's the song about?

It's about the unease about the world — the feeling of the barbarians already being inside the gates. Kind of like the fall of something beautiful. The song is celebrating that beauty.

## How did it come together in the studio/production?

It's instrumental. There's a vocal sound but it's actually an instrument that just sounds vocal-like. I like a slightly rough bass combined with prettier overtones, and that's what I went for here. I also used moving drum sounds to vary it up and make it interesting.

## Favourite moment in the track

I like when the "hey" and "woow" kick off the more exciting part of the song.

## One thing listeners might miss

I bring in the bass in a more subtle way at the beginning to give the listener a clue that it will come in stronger later. See if you hear it!
